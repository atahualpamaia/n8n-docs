Inbound Leads Qualification

This workflow automates qualification and enrichment of inbound leads by integrating multiple validation and data enrichment services. It verifies emails, phone numbers, and company information, checks for duplicates, and creates or updates records in Airtable and Pipedrive. The goal is to ensure only high-quality leads progress to the sales pipeline, saving time and improving conversion rates.

Execution Flow
1
Lead Intake — Captures and restores raw inbound lead data from a webhook trigger.
2
Data Verification — Validates and enriches email, phone, and company details using external APIs and OpenAI checks.
3
Qualification — Assesses ICP fit, collects validation results, and assigns a qualification status.
4
CRM & Enrichment — Creates or updates lead, person, and organization records in Airtable and Pipedrive, and coordinates enrichment with Clay.
5
Deduplication & Finalization — Checks for duplicates in Pipedrive and marks or updates records accordingly.
Technical Notes

Maintain API keys and monitor for API rate limits; periodic updates may be needed when underlying service schemas change.
