Inbound Leads Qualification

This workflow automates the qualification of inbound leads using a series of checks and validations. It efficiently processes lead information to determine readiness for sales engagement and captures relevant data in Airtable and Pipedrive.

Execution Flow
1
Data Reception — The workflow starts by receiving lead data via a webhook.
2
Data Processing & Validation — Various checks and verifications are applied to the lead data to assess quality and readiness.
3
Lead Creation — Qualified leads are created as records in Pipedrive and relevant details are saved in Airtable.
Technical Notes

Ensure to monitor the webhook for responses and manage API limits for external services to avoid bottlenecks.
